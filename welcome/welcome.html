YT Studio Like/Dislike Ratio

Already on

Nothing to switch on

The extension is active automatically right after installation. You don't need to enable anything — just open YouTube Studio.

Where the data appears

Go to YouTube Studio and open the Content tab (the video list). Two extra columns are added on the right: Likes (vs. Dislikes) and Subscribers (total). The values load within a moment — reload the tab if nothing shows up.

Open YouTube Studio

Turn it on or off

Click the extension icon in the browser toolbar to open the settings popup. There you can toggle the display on/off. It is on by default. (Tip: pin the icon so it's always visible.)

Something missing? Send a report

YouTube sometimes changes its internal data without notice. If a column stays empty, the Debug mode helps me fix it:

  1. Click the extension icon and switch on Debug mode.
  2. Reload the Content tab and open the developer console with F12.
  3. Filter the console for [YTSR].
  4. Copy the output and send it to me via the GitHub issue tracker.

Open the issue tracker

Your data stays with you

Everything runs inside your own signed-in session. The extension shows only your own channel's numbers and sends nothing to any server or third party.
